But what does it truly mean ?

 

Embodiment is to actually live something out.

To exist in something.

To exist in who you are.

Feminine embodiment is to live in YOUR feminine. The ability to live in a way that nourishes YOUR unique feminine energy. This simply means truly becoming YOU. coming home to YOURSELF.

Not that of her on instagram

Of the one in the field eating fruit or laying by the pool in a big fuck off hat .(.unless that is your truth of course )

The major cavern to a woman existing in her innate empower is actually the weight of unresolved trauma held in her body.

We are conditioned ( and required ) to suppress our desires, quieten down our voices, and systematically place everyone else's needs before our own.

This is not just in intimate and family relationships..

We are required to pretend we do not exist in a moon governed body so we can partake in a sun governed society.

Many of us have learned to exist in a shell of who we truly are, disconnected from our bodies and our inherent feminine wisdom. We are constantly informed of our lack, of our wrongness and of the dooming obsolete-ness waiting for us once we can't produce babies as we age.

A woman's disconnection from herself stems from layers of cultural and personal trauma stored deep within our physical being.

Your path to authentic feminine embodiment isn't about becoming someone else – it's about coming home to yourself. But how can we truly come home when our bodies are holding onto years of stored trauma in our myofascial tissue? And our wombs are holding generational trauma.

To be human is to know trauma.

To be a woman is to carry the load of personal, family and generational trauma.

My loves… the truth is we agreed to do this.

We are so damn powerful as women we birthed the whole god damn nation, kept them fed and our world exists because we bought them here… lets not forget…

But we don't need to emanate the religious bullying of a man on a cross carrying the sins of all… we do not live in such repression of trauma..that is the patriarch’s need so we don't have time or energy to remember and exist in our powerful embodiment.

And we also don't have time to do months and years of talk therapy.

 

 

Years ago when I was busy saving my life by being pulled through drug rehabilitation I got to a point where I felt I was in a loop of the sameness. Talking, talking and talking about the past.

I was conflicted as at the beginning it was so incredibly profound to me to be able to put words to what I had felt and dealt with for decades and to have someone listen and not judge me.

Yet I reached a point where I felt I was not moving forward. It was as if I found a hard and uncomfortable bench to sit on..which kept me off the ground yet somehow was holding me back also.

I had lived in these patterns for over 25 yrs…

  • Pushing down my needs because I was labeled ( way ) "too much"

  • I was living in a constant state of stress and adrenaline chase, because it feels safer than slowing down (I even said to my therapist that I would do anything she asked as long as she did not take away my highs)

  • I was carrying a deep distrust of men, even a hatred that had become my armor

  • I was totally disconnected from my femininity, having built a resilience in order to keep working in the gold mining industry and male dominated arenas.

  • I totally resented my monthly cycle

  • And I was always being told I was "too loud," "too needy," "too emotional" so many times that I just believed it to be true.

Maybe you recognize yourself in some of these patterns.

Even though I had worked on so many of these unintegrated parts of me ( especially the hatred and mistrust towards men ) the others still lingered inconveniently sabotaging so many aspects of my life.
